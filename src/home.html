March 8, 2017 | Champions League

FC Barcelona 6-1 Paris Saint-Germain

The greatest comeback in Champions League history, Barcelona overturned a 4-0 first leg deficit with a dramatic 6-1 victory. Sergi Roberto's 95th-minute winner sent Camp Nou into a frenzy as Barça completed the impossible.
